Zenith Rijal

Hey, I'm Zenith. I am currently a student navigating my Civil Engineering course at Shree Bhanubhakta Secondary School in Deumai-4, Ilam, Nepal.

This is my creative platform. While I am firmly grounded in my student journey, I use this space to actively expand my graphic design knowledge and take on freelance projects. I don't claim to be an old industry veteran, but I bring immense focus, energy, and a fresh artistic vision to every commission I tackle.

I blend structural precision with raw, alternative design choices to make visual content stand out from the noise.
